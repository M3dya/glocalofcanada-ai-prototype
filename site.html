Programs · CANConnect

Up to $5,000 for your community project

CANConnect Microgrants empower Canadian youth — especially underserved youth — to launch creative civic engagement projects nationwide.

Apply nowBook an info session
What it is

160 microgrants for 160 youth

Funded by the Canada Service Corps at Employment and Social Development Canada and administered by GLOCAL, the program distributes microgrants to youth across the country, with mentorship and digital civic training to help you carry out your initiative.

How to apply

From idea to funded in three steps

1

Shape your idea

Pick a local, regional, or community concern and outline a project that addresses it.

2

Submit your application

Complete the CANConnect application form with your plan and budget.

3

Get mentored & build

Receive funding plus guidance from the GLOCAL team to bring your project to life.

Common questions

FAQs

Who can volunteer?
Canadian youth aged 12–30 looking for a flexible way to make a difference and build skills.
How many hours do I need?
The full program is 120 hours across three modules, completed at your own pace before December 2026.
What do I get at the end?
A digital certificate for 120 verified hours signed by the Minister of Jobs and Families, plus a skill portfolio and priority access to microgrants and summer jobs.
Do I have to commit to a fixed schedule?
No. You mix and match activities based on your availability — some bite-sized tasks take as little as 15 minutes.
How do I log my hours?
From your profile dashboard on the YouCount platform, where you can track progress toward your certificate.

About

Who we are

GLOCAL, the GLOCAL Foundation of Canada, is a national not-for-profit promoting digital civic engagement through local participation and global understanding.

Our vision

A Canada where every voice shapes the future

We believe no one's potential should go untapped. Through volunteering and innovative initiatives, you can give back, build transferable skills, and bring passion projects to life — on your own terms.

Our mission

Connect local actions with a global perspective

The term GLOCAL captures it: act locally, think globally. We connect young Canadians' efforts to a broader civic engagement framework and a national network of support.

Our team

Built by young Canadians

A passionate, diverse team brought together by a vision of a more inclusive, accessible, and engaging Canadian society — supported by a board of directors including academic leaders.
